D - Interactive

Time limit per test: 1 seconds

Memory limit per test: 1 GB

I am thinking of a number between 1 and 100. You may ask me a number, and I will tell you if the number I am thinking of is higher, lower or equal to your guess. Once you figure out the number, print out ! x, where x is your guess.

Input

Use standard input to read responses. The input will contain responses to your queries. Make sure to flush the output after each of your questions. The response will be higher if the number is greater than your number, lower if it is less than your number, and eqal if it is equal. The number I am thinking of is 1 <= n <= 100

Output

Use standard output to make queries. Queries are of the form ? x, where x is your guess. Once you have the number, print ! x and terminate your program.

Examples

    ? 3
    higher
    ? 7
    lower
    ? 5
    equal
    ! 5
